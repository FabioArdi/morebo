About MoReBo

MoReBo is a movie recommendation bot that was created as project at ZHAW in the module Python-Grundlagen & Anwendung in Data Science.

MoReBo uses machine learning algorithms to analyze the movies you like and recommend similar movies that you'll love. It's like having your own personal movie curator!
